Lingo Lock

This fun project is the result of a 10 hour assignment to develop something that will provide a user with the right amount of discomfort in pursuit of helping them achieve a bigger goal. The purpose of the Lingo Lock is to lock the user's computer after it has been woken up from a sleep mode. This additional lock screen requires the user to answer a vocab question from one of their current Duolingo courses in order to unlock their computer.
